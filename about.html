Who are you?

Escapists Productions was founded by Nathanael Ogden and Charlotte Anderson. together the two of them set out to shape the film industry to make way for young people to enter into a safe environment where they can practice their skills and become well versed in the industry. Together Nathanael and Charlotte have worked tirelessly to create their brand.

What can we do for you?

We help our clients with everything from pre to post-production. We function as a liaison between all your required services.

How Can a Production Company Help You?

We will finance and produce your film for you. Our producers are some of the best in biz and can give you all you need to ensure a happy and worry-free project. We will handle all your logistics.
